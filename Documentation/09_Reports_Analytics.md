# 📈 Reports & Analytics

Make smarter decisions with real-time **Reports** and **Analytics**. See what's working, what's not, and where to focus your energy.

---

## Accessing Reports

1. Click **Reports** in the sidebar.
2. Choose a report type from the available categories.

---

## Available Reports

### 💰 Sales Reports
| Report | What It Shows |
|--------|-------------|
| **Revenue Summary** | Total revenue this month, quarter, and year |
| **Deal Conversion** | How many leads turned into deals, and deals into wins |
| **Pipeline Value** | Total value of deals at each stage |

| Report | What It Shows |

| Report | What It Shows |

## Understanding the Dashboard Charts

### Bar Charts
- Show comparisons (e.g., revenue by month).
- Taller bars = higher values.

### Line Charts
- Show trends over time (e.g., leads per week).
- Going up = growth. Going down = decline.

### Pie Charts
- Show proportions (e.g., lead sources).
- Bigger slices = larger share.

### Numbers (KPI Cards)
- Show key metrics at a glance.
- Green arrow ↑ = improvement.
- Red arrow ↓ = needs attention.

---

## Filtering Reports

Most reports let you filter by:
- **Date Range** — This week, this month, custom range
- **Agent/User** — Specific team member
- **Source** — Where leads came from
- **Status** — Lead or deal status

### To Filter:
1. Click the **Filter** button on any report.
2. Set your criteria.
3. Click **Apply**.
4. The report updates instantly.

---

## Exporting Reports

Need to share a report with your boss or team?

1. Click the **Export** button on any report.
2. Choose format:
   - **CSV** — For Excel/Google Sheets
   - **PDF** — For printing or emailing
3. The file downloads to your computer.

---

## Tips

1. **Check reports weekly** — Make it a habit every Monday morning.
2. **Focus on trends** — One bad day is normal. A bad trend needs action.
3. **Compare periods** — This month vs. last month shows growth.
4. **Share insights** — Reports are useless if nobody sees them.
