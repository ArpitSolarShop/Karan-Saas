# ❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Quick answers to common questions about Project Alpha CRM.

---

## General

### Q: What is Project Alpha?
**A:** Project Alpha is an all-in-one CRM (Customer Relationship Management) platform. It helps you manage leads, track sales, communicate with customers, send invoices, and run your entire business from one place.

### Q: Do I need to install anything?
**A:** No! Project Alpha works in your web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Edge, Safari). Just open the link and log in.

### Q: Can I use it on my phone?
**A:** Yes! The interface is responsive and works on mobile browsers. Open the same link on your phone's browser.

### Q: Is my data secure?
**A:** Yes. All data is encrypted and stored securely. Only your team can access your company's data.

## Login & Account

### Q: I forgot my password. What do I do?
**A:** Contact your administrator. They can reset your password from the admin panel.

### Q: Can I change my email address?
**A:** Contact your administrator to update your email.

### Q: I keep getting logged out. Why?
**A:** For security, sessions expire after a period of inactivity. Simply log in again.

## Leads

### Q: What's the difference between a Lead and a Contact?
**A:** A **Lead** is someone who might buy from you. Once they buy, they become a **Customer/Contact**. In Project Alpha, leads include all potential and existing customers.

### Q: Can I import leads from Excel?
**A:** Yes! Go to Leads → click Import → select your Excel (.xlsx) or CSV file. Make sure it has columns for Name and Phone.

### Q: How do I avoid duplicate leads?
**A:** The system checks for duplicate phone numbers during import. You can also use the DNC (Do Not Call) list to manage contacts.

### Q: What does "Lead Score" mean?
**A:** It's a number (0-100) showing how likely a lead is to buy. Higher = more likely. It's based on their activity and engagement.

## Deals

### Q: Can I customize the deal stages?
**A:** The default stages (Prospecting → Qualify → Proposal → Negotiate → Won/Lost) work for most businesses. Ask your administrator to customize them.

### Q: What happens when I move a deal to "Won"?
**A:** Congratulations! The deal value is added to your revenue. You can then create an invoice for the customer.

### Q: Can multiple people work on the same deal?
**A:** Each deal has one **Owner**, but all team members can view it and add notes.

## Communication

### Q: Can I make phone calls from the CRM?
**A:** Yes! Use the **Dialer** feature. Your administrator needs to set up telephony first.

### Q: Why can't I send WhatsApp messages to new contacts?
**A:** WhatsApp requires you to use pre-approved **templates** when messaging someone for the first time. After they reply, you can send regular messages.

### Q: Are WhatsApp messages saved?
**A:** Yes! All messages are automatically saved in the CRM and linked to the lead's profile.

---

## Invoices & Payments

### Q: Can customers pay online?
**A:** If your administrator has set up a payment gateway (like Razorpay), customers can pay online via the invoice link.

### Q: Can I customize the invoice template?
**A:** Ask your administrator to set up your company logo, address, and tax details in Settings.

### Q: How do I handle partial payments?
**A:** Record the partial amount. The invoice status stays "Pending" until the full amount is paid.

## Troubleshooting

### Q: The page is loading slowly. What should I do?
**A:** 
1. Check your internet connection.
2. Try refreshing the page (Ctrl + R or Cmd + R).
3. Clear your browser cache.
4. Try a different browser.

### Q: I see an error message. What should I do?
**A:**
1. Take a screenshot of the error.
2. Note what you were doing when it happened.
3. Contact your administrator with the screenshot.

### Q: My changes aren't saving. Why?
**A:**
1. Check your internet connection.
2. Make sure you clicked the **Save** button.
3. Try refreshing the page and trying again.
4. Contact your administrator if the issue persists.
