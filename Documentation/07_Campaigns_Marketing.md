# 🎯 Campaigns & Marketing

Run marketing campaigns to reach your customers at scale — via phone, email, or WhatsApp.

---

## What Is a Campaign?

A **Campaign** is an organized effort to contact a group of leads. For example:
- Call all leads from Jaipur who signed up this month.
- Send a WhatsApp message to all "Hot Leads".
- Email a Diwali discount offer to all existing customers.

---

## Creating a Campaign

1. Click **Campaigns** in the sidebar.
2. Click **+ New Campaign**.
3. Fill in:
   - **Name** — Give it a clear name (e.g., "March Cold Calls")
   - **Type** — Outbound Call, WhatsApp, or Email
   - **Lead List** — Select which group of leads to target
   - **Script** — (For calls) The talking points for agents
   - **Start Date** — When the campaign begins
4. Click **Create**.

---

## Campaign Types

### 📞 Outbound Calling Campaign
- Assigns leads to your agents.
- Agents see leads in their **Dialer** queue.
- Auto-logs call outcomes (duration, disposition, notes).

### 💬 WhatsApp Campaign
- Sends a template message to all leads in the list.
- Great for announcements, offers, and reminders.
- Requires a WhatsApp-approved template.

### ✉️ Email Campaign
- Sends emails to a list of leads.
- You can schedule emails to send at a specific time.
- Track who opened and clicked.

## Managing Active Campaigns

### Campaign Dashboard
- **Total Leads** — How many leads are in the campaign
- **Contacted** — How many have been reached
- **Interested** — How many showed interest
- **Converted** — How many became customers

### Monitoring Progress
1. Click on any active campaign.
2. See real-time progress bars for each metric.
3. View individual lead statuses.

## Lead Lists

Lead lists are groups of leads used in campaigns.

### Creating a Lead List
1. Go to **Leads** page.
2. Use **Filters** to narrow down (e.g., City = "Mumbai", Status = "New").
3. Select the leads you want.
4. Click **Create List** and give it a name (e.g., "Mumbai New Leads Jan").
5. This list is now available when creating campaigns.

### Importing a Lead List
1. Click **Import** in the Leads page.
2. Upload an Excel or CSV file.
3. The imported leads automatically form a list.

---

## DNC (Do Not Call) List

### What Is DNC?
If a customer says "don't call me again", you must respect that. The **DNC list** prevents anyone from accidentally contacting them.

### Adding to DNC
1. Go to **DNC** in the sidebar.
2. Click **+ Add Number**.
3. Enter the phone number.
4. That number is now blocked from all campaigns.

> ⚠️ **Important:** Calling DNC numbers may violate regulations. Always respect customer preferences.

## Tips for Great Campaigns

1. **Segment your leads** — Don't send the same message to everyone.
2. **Personalize** — Use the customer's name. "Hi Rahul!" works better than "Hi Customer!".
3. **Time it right** — Don't call at 7 AM or 10 PM.
4. **Test first** — Send to a small group before scaling up.
5. **Track results** — Use the campaign dashboard to see what works.
