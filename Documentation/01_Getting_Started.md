# 🚀 Getting Started with Project Alpha CRM

Welcome to **Project Alpha** — your all-in-one business management platform. This guide will help you get up and running in under 10 minutes.

---

## Step 1: Log In

1. Open your browser and go to the link provided by your administrator (e.g., `https://yourcompany.projectalpha.com`).
2. You will see the **Welcome Back** login screen.
3. Enter your **Email** and **Password** provided by your admin.
4. Click the purple **Sign In** button.
5. You can also click **Sign in with Google** if your company uses Google Workspace.

> 💡 **Tip:** If you forget your password, contact your administrator to reset it.

---

## Step 2: Understanding the Dashboard

Once logged in, you will see the **Main Dashboard**. This is your command center.

### The Sidebar (Left Panel)
The left side of the screen has icons for every section of the CRM:

| Icon | Section | What It Does |
|------|---------|-------------|
| 📊 | **Dashboard** | Overview of your key metrics and activity |
| 👥 | **Leads** | All your potential customers |
| 💰 | **Deals** | Your sales pipeline (Kanban board) |
| 📋 | **Tasks** | Your to-do list and assignments |
| 🎯 | **Campaigns** | Marketing campaigns and outreach |
| 📞 | **Dialer** | Make and receive phone calls |
| 💬 | **WhatsApp** | Chat with customers via WhatsApp |
| 🧾 | **Invoices** | Create and track invoices |
| 📦 | **Products** | Your product catalog |
| 📁 | **Documents** | File storage and management |
| 📅 | **Calendar** | Schedule meetings and events |
| 🎫 | **Tickets** | Customer support tickets |
| 📈 | **Reports** | Analytics and performance reports |

---

## Step 3: Your First Actions

### Add Your First Lead
1. Click **Leads** in the sidebar.
2. Click the **+ Add Lead** button (top-right).
3. Fill in the customer's **Name**, **Phone**, and **Email**.
4. Click **Save**.

### Create Your First Deal
1. Click **Deals** in the sidebar.
2. Click **+ New Deal**.
3. Give it a **Title** (e.g., "Acme Corp Website Redesign").
4. Set the **Amount** (e.g., ₹50,000).
5. Click **Save**.
6. Your deal will appear as a card in the **Prospecting** column.

### Make Your First Call
1. Click **Dialer** in the sidebar.
2. Type or paste the customer's phone number.
3. Click the green **Call** button.
4. After the call, add notes about what was discussed.

---

## Step 4: Customize Your Profile

1. Click the **Settings** icon (gear) at the bottom of the sidebar.
2. Update your **Name**, **Phone**, and **Profile Picture**.
3. Click **Save Changes**.
