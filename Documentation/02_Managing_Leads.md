# 👥 Managing Leads

Leads are potential customers who have shown interest in your product or service. This guide explains how to add, organize, and convert them into paying customers.

---

## What Is a Lead?

A **Lead** is a person or company that could become your customer. For example:
- Someone who filled out a form on your website.
- A person you met at a trade show.
- A referral from an existing customer.

---

## Viewing Your Leads

1. Click **Leads** in the sidebar.
2. You will see a table showing all your leads with columns like:
   - **Name** — The lead's full name
   - **Phone** — Their phone number
   - **Email** — Their email address
   - **Status** — Where they are in your process (New, Contacted, Qualified, etc.)
   - **Source** — How they found you (Website, Referral, Cold Call, etc.)
   - **Score** — A number showing how likely they are to buy (higher = better)

---

## Adding a New Lead

### Manually
1. Click the **+ Add Lead** button.
2. Fill in at least the **Name** and **Phone Number**.
3. Optionally add: Email, Company, Source, City, Tags.
4. Click **Save**.

### Import from Excel/CSV
1. Click the **Import** button (upload icon).
2. Select your Excel (.xlsx) or CSV file.
3. The system will automatically map columns like Name, Phone, and Email.
4. Click **Import** to add all leads at once.

> 💡 **Tip:** Your file should have columns named "Name", "Phone", and "Email" for best results.

---

## Lead Statuses

Each lead goes through stages:

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| 🆕 **New** | Just added, not yet contacted |
| 📞 **Contacted** | You've reached out to them |
| ✅ **Qualified** | They're a good fit and interested |
| 💰 **Converted** | They became a paying customer! |
| ❌ **Lost** | They decided not to buy |
| 🚫 **DNC** | Do Not Contact — they asked not to be called |

### Changing a Lead's Status
1. Click on a lead to open their profile.
2. Click the **Status** dropdown.
3. Select the new status.
4. The change is saved automatically.

---

## Lead Actions

### Call a Lead
- Click the **📞 Phone** icon next to any lead to start a call.

### Send WhatsApp
- Click the **💬 WhatsApp** icon to open a chat.

### Add Notes
1. Open a lead's profile.
2. Scroll to the **Notes** section.
3. Type your note and click **Add Note**.
4. Notes are visible to your entire team.

### Add Tags
- Tags help you organize leads (e.g., "Hot Lead", "Follow Up Friday", "VIP").
- Click **+ Add Tag** on any lead's profile.

---

## Searching and Filtering

### Search
- Use the **Search bar** at the top to find leads by name, phone, or email.

### Filter
- Click the **Filter** button to narrow down by:
  - Status (e.g., show only "New" leads)
  - Source (e.g., show only "Website" leads)
  - Tags (e.g., show only "VIP" leads)
  - Date range (e.g., leads added this week)

---

## Bulk Actions

Need to update many leads at once?

1. Select multiple leads using the **checkboxes** on the left.
2. Click the **Bulk Actions** dropdown:
   - **Assign** — Assign all selected leads to a team member.
   - **Tag** — Add a tag to all selected leads.
   - **Change Status** — Update all their statuses at once.
   - **Delete** — Remove all selected leads.

---

## Exporting Leads

1. Click the **Export** button.
2. Choose your format (CSV).
3. The file will download to your computer.

> 💡 **Tip:** You can filter leads first, then export only the filtered results.

---

## Converting a Lead to a Deal

When a lead is ready to buy:
1. Open the lead's profile.
2. Click **Convert to Deal**.
3. Enter the deal **Title** and **Amount**.
4. The lead automatically moves to your **Deals Pipeline**.

---

*Remember: Every customer starts as a lead. The better you manage your leads, the more sales you'll close!* 🎯
