# 💬 WhatsApp & Communication

Project Alpha lets you chat with customers directly through **WhatsApp** — right from your CRM. No switching between apps!

---

## Setting Up WhatsApp

> ⚠️ **One-time setup required.** Ask your administrator to connect your business WhatsApp number.

Once connected, you'll see the **WhatsApp** icon in your sidebar.

---

## Sending Messages

### From the WhatsApp Page
1. Click **WhatsApp** in the sidebar.
2. You'll see your **Inbox** — a list of all conversations.
3. Click on a contact to open their chat.
4. Type your message at the bottom and press **Enter** or click **Send**.

### From a Lead's Profile
1. Open any lead's profile.
2. Click the **💬 WhatsApp** icon.
3. A chat window opens with that contact.
4. Type and send your message.

---

## Message Types

You can send:
- **Text messages** — Regular typed messages.
- **Templates** — Pre-written messages approved by WhatsApp (great for first contact).
- **Images** — Product photos, brochures, etc.
- **Documents** — PDFs, quotes, invoices.

---

## Templates

Templates are pre-approved messages that you can send to customers who haven't messaged you first. Examples:

| Template Name | Use Case |
|--------------|----------|
| Welcome | First contact with a new lead |
| Follow Up | Checking in after a meeting |
| Quote Sent | Notification that a quote was sent |
| Payment Reminder | Gentle payment nudge |

### Using a Template
1. In any chat, click the **📋 Templates** button.
2. Select a template.
3. Fill in any variable fields (like customer name or amount).
4. Click **Send**.

---

## Quick Replies

For messages you send frequently, create **Quick Replies**:
1. Go to **Settings** → **WhatsApp** → **Quick Replies**.
2. Click **+ Add**.
3. Type a shortcut (e.g., `/thanks`) and the full message.
4. In any chat, type `/thanks` and the full message auto-fills.

---

## Phone Calls (Dialer)

### Making a Call
1. Click **Dialer** in the sidebar.
2. Enter the phone number or select a lead.
3. Click the green **📞 Call** button.
4. Your call starts!

### During a Call
- **Mute** — Click the microphone icon.
- **Hold** — Click the pause icon.
- **Transfer** — Send the call to a colleague.
- **End** — Click the red phone icon.

### After a Call
- Add **Disposition** (outcome): Interested, Not Interested, Callback, etc.
- Type **Notes** about what was discussed.
- Schedule a **Follow-up** if needed.

---

## Email

### Sending Emails
1. Open a lead's profile.
2. Click the **✉️ Email** tab.
3. Write your subject and message.
4. Click **Send**.

### Email Templates
- Use pre-built templates for common emails (welcome, proposal, follow-up).
- Templates can include variables like `{{name}}` and `{{company}}` that auto-fill.

---

## Tips

1. **Respond quickly** — Customers expect fast replies on WhatsApp (within 1-2 hours).
2. **Use templates for first contact** — WhatsApp requires templates for initiating conversations.
3. **Log everything** — All messages are saved in the CRM, so your team can see the full history.
4. **Be professional** — WhatsApp messages represent your business.
