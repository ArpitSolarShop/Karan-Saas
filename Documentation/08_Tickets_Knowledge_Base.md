# 🎫 Support Tickets & Knowledge Base

Provide excellent customer support with the built-in **Ticketing System** and self-service **Knowledge Base**.

---

## Support Tickets

### What Is a Ticket?
A **Ticket** is a customer's request for help. Examples:
- "My order hasn't arrived yet"
- "I have a question about pricing"
- "There's a bug in the software"

### Viewing Tickets
1. Click **Tickets** in the sidebar.
2. See all tickets in a table with:
   - **Subject** — What the issue is about
   - **Status** — Open, In Progress, Resolved, Closed
   - **Priority** — Low, Medium, High, Urgent
   - **Assigned To** — Who's handling it
   - **Created** — When the ticket was raised

### Creating a Ticket
1. Click **+ New Ticket**.
2. Fill in:
   - **Subject** — Brief description of the issue
   - **Description** — Full details
   - **Priority** — How urgent it is
   - **Category** — Type of issue (Billing, Technical, General, etc.)
3. Click **Create**.

### Responding to a Ticket
1. Click on a ticket to open it.
2. Read the customer's message.
3. Type your reply in the **Response** box.
4. Click **Send Reply**.
5. The customer receives your response via email.

### Resolving a Ticket
1. Once the issue is fixed, change the status to **Resolved**.
2. The customer gets a notification.
3. If they confirm the fix, change status to **Closed**.

## Ticket Priorities

| Priority | Response Time | Examples |
|----------|--------------|---------|
| 🔴 **Urgent** | Within 1 hour | System down, payment issue |
| 🟠 **High** | Within 4 hours | Feature broken, missing data |
| 🟡 **Medium** | Within 1 day | Question, minor issue |
| 🟢 **Low** | Within 3 days | Feature request, general inquiry |

## Knowledge Base

### What Is the Knowledge Base?
It's a library of articles that help customers (and your team) find answers without creating a ticket.

### Viewing Articles
1. Click **Knowledge Base** (📚) in the sidebar.
2. Browse articles by category.
3. Use the **Search bar** to find specific topics.

### Creating an Article
1. Click **+ New Article**.
2. Fill in:
   - **Title** — Clear and searchable (e.g., "How to Reset Your Password")
   - **Category** — Group (e.g., "Account", "Billing", "Technical")
   - **Content** — The full article with step-by-step instructions
3. Click **Publish**.

### Article Tips
- Use **simple language** — Remember, your readers are not technical.
- Include **screenshots** when possible.
- Add **step-by-step instructions** (1, 2, 3...).
- Update articles regularly.

## Tips for Great Support

1. **Respond fast** — A quick response shows you care.
2. **Be empathetic** — "I understand your frustration" goes a long way.
3. **Solve the root cause** — Don't just fix the symptom.
4. **Create KB articles** — If you answer the same question twice, make it an article.
5. **Follow up** — Check back to make sure the issue stayed resolved.
