# 🧾 Invoices, Quotes & Products

Manage your entire sales cycle — from quoting a price to collecting payment.

---

## Products (Your Catalog)

### What Is the Product Catalog?
It's your list of everything you sell — products, services, or packages.

### Viewing Products
1. Click **Products** in the sidebar.
2. See all your products in a table with **Name**, **Price**, **Category**, and **Stock**.

### Adding a Product
1. Click **+ Add Product**.
2. Fill in:
   - **Name** — e.g., "Solar Panel 330W"
   - **SKU** — Product code (e.g., "SP-330W")
   - **Price** — Selling price
   - **Category** — Group (e.g., "Panels", "Inverters")
   - **Description** — Details about the product
3. Click **Save**.

---

## Quotes (Price Proposals)

### What Is a Quote?
A **Quote** is a formal price proposal you send to a customer before they buy. It lists items, quantities, and prices.

### Creating a Quote
1. Open a **Deal** from your pipeline.
2. Click **Create Quote**.
3. Add line items:
   - Select products from your catalog.
   - Set **Quantity**.
   - Adjust **Discount** if needed.
4. The system automatically calculates:
   - **Subtotal**
   - **Tax** (GST/VAT)
   - **Grand Total**
5. Click **Save** or **Send to Customer**.

### Quote Statuses
| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| 📝 Draft | Not yet sent |
| ✉️ Sent | Sent to the customer |
| ✅ Approved | Customer accepted the price |
| ❌ Rejected | Customer declined |
| ⏰ Expired | Quote validity ended |

## Invoices (Payment Requests)

### What Is an Invoice?
An **Invoice** is a formal bill you send to a customer after they agree to buy.

### Creating an Invoice
1. Click **Invoices** in the sidebar.
2. Click **+ New Invoice**.
3. Fill in:
   - **Customer / Company** — Who to bill
   - **Invoice Date** — Today's date (auto-filled)
   - **Due Date** — When payment is expected
   - **Line Items** — Products/services with quantities and prices
4. The system calculates the total.
5. Click **Save** or **Send**.

### Invoice Statuses
| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| 📝 Draft | Not yet finalized |
| ✉️ Sent | Sent to customer |
| ⏳ Pending | Awaiting payment |
| ✅ Paid | Payment received! |
| ⚠️ Overdue | Payment is late |
| ❌ Cancelled | Invoice was voided |

### Recording a Payment
1. Open an invoice.
2. Click **Record Payment**.
3. Enter the **Amount**, **Date**, and **Payment Method** (Cash, UPI, Bank Transfer, etc.).
4. Click **Save**.
5. If fully paid, the status changes to **Paid ✅**.

Here's how everything connects:

```
Product Catalog
      ↓
  Create Quote  →  Send to Customer
      ↓
Customer Approves
      ↓
  Create Invoice  →  Send to Customer
      ↓
  Record Payment
      ↓
   Deal Won! 🎉
```

## Tips

1. **Keep your product catalog updated** — Accurate prices prevent misquotes.
2. **Send quotes quickly** — Speed wins deals.
3. **Follow up on pending invoices** — Set tasks to remind yourself.
4. **Use invoice numbers** — The system auto-generates them for easy tracking.
