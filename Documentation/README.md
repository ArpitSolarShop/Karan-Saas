## 🚀 Quick Start (2 Minutes)

If you're in a hurry, here's all you need:

1. **Log in** with your email and password at the link provided by your admin.
2. **Click "Leads"** in the sidebar → Click **"+ Add Lead"** → Fill in name & phone → Click **Save**.
3. **Click "Deals"** in the sidebar → Click **"+ New Deal"** → Set title & amount → Click **Save**.
4. **Drag your deal** from "Prospecting" to "Won" when you close the sale. 🎉

That's it! You're selling. Read the detailed guides above when you have more time.

## 💡 Top Tips for Success

1. **Update the CRM after every interaction** — Calls, meetings, emails. Log everything.
2. **Use tasks and follow-ups** — Never forget a promise to a customer.
3. **Check your dashboard daily** — Know your numbers.
4. **Be consistent** — 10 minutes of CRM updates daily saves hours of confusion later.
5. **Ask for help** — Your team and administrator are here to support you.
