# 💰 Deals Pipeline & Sales

The **Deals Pipeline** is a visual board that shows where each of your sales opportunities stands. Think of it like a Kanban board for your money!

---

## What Is a Deal?

A **Deal** is a sales opportunity — a potential sale that you're working on. Each deal has:
- A **Title** (e.g., "Acme Corp Annual Contract")
- An **Amount** (e.g., ₹1,50,000)
- A **Stage** (where it is in your sales process)
- An **Owner** (the salesperson responsible)

---

## The Pipeline View (Kanban Board)

When you click **Deals** in the sidebar, you see columns representing each stage:

```
| Prospecting | Qualify | Proposal | Negotiate | Won ✓ | Lost ✗ |
|    Card 1   | Card 3  |  Card 5  |  Card 7   |       |        |
|    Card 2   | Card 4  |  Card 6  |           |       |        |
```

Each **card** is one deal. You can see the deal name and amount right on the card.

---

## Deal Stages Explained

| Stage | What Happens Here |
|-------|-------------------|
| **Prospecting** | You've identified a potential customer but haven't qualified them yet |
| **Qualify** | You've confirmed they need your product and have the budget |
| **Proposal** | You've sent them a quote or proposal |
| **Negotiate** | You're discussing terms, pricing, or contracts |
| **Won ✓** | Congratulations! The deal is closed and you made the sale! |
| **Lost ✗** | The deal didn't work out (that's okay, learn from it) |

---

## Creating a New Deal

1. Click the **+ New Deal** button (top-right).
2. Fill in:
   - **Deal Name** — A clear name (e.g., "Website Redesign for Smith & Co")
   - **Amount** — The value of the deal
   - **Stage** — Where it starts (usually "Prospecting")
3. Click **Create**.
4. Your deal card appears in the pipeline!

---

## Moving a Deal Between Stages

### Drag and Drop
Simply **click and hold** a deal card, then **drag it** to the next column. For example:
- Drag from **Prospecting** → **Qualify** when you've confirmed interest.
- Drag from **Negotiate** → **Won** when the deal is closed!

### Click to Update
1. Click on a deal card to open its details.
2. Change the **Stage** dropdown.
3. Changes save automatically.

---

## Key Metrics at the Top

The pipeline header shows you important numbers:
- **📊 Pipeline Value** — Total value of all active deals
- **⚡ Forecast** — Expected revenue (based on probability)
- **💰 Won** — Total value of closed deals

---

## Deal Details

Click any deal card to see its full profile:

- **Timeline** — History of all activities and updates
- **Notes** — Team notes and comments
- **Tasks** — Action items related to this deal
- **Attachments** — Files like proposals, contracts, or images
- **Quotes** — Price quotations sent to the customer

---

## Tips for Success

1. **Update deals regularly** — Move them through stages as conversations progress.
2. **Add notes after every interaction** — Your future self will thank you.
3. **Set expected close dates** — This helps with revenue forecasting.
4. **Don't be afraid of "Lost"** — It clears your pipeline and keeps you focused on real opportunities.

---

*Your pipeline is your revenue engine. Keep it clean, keep it moving!* 🚀
