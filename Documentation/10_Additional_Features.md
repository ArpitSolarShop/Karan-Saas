# ⚙️ Additional Features

Project Alpha has many more powerful features beyond the basics. Here's a quick guide to each one.

---

## 📁 Documents & File Vault

Store and organize all your business files in one place.

### How to Use
1. Click **Documents** in the sidebar.
2. **Create Folders** — Organize files by client, project, or type.
3. **Upload Files** — Click **+ Upload** to add PDFs, images, spreadsheets, etc.
4. **Search** — Find any file instantly by name.

### Tips
- Create a folder for each major client.
- Upload contracts, proposals, and receipts here.
- Your team can access shared files.

---

## 🏢 Companies

Track the organizations you do business with.

### How to Use
1. Click **Companies** in the sidebar.
2. Click **+ Add Company** to create a new one.
3. Fill in: Name, Industry, Website, Address, Size.
4. Link **Leads** and **Deals** to the company.

### Why It Matters
- Multiple leads can belong to one company.
- See all deals with a company at a glance.
- Track company-level revenue.

---

## 📦 Projects

Track long-term projects with milestones.

### How to Use
1. Click **Projects** in the sidebar.
2. Click **+ New Project** to create one.
3. Add **Milestones** — Key deliverables with deadlines.
4. Track progress as milestones are completed.

---

## ✅ Approvals

Some actions require manager approval before proceeding.

### How It Works
1. When you mark a deal as "Won" or create a large discount, an **Approval Request** is sent to your manager.
2. Your manager sees it in their **Approvals** inbox.
3. They can **Approve** ✅ or **Reject** ❌ with comments.
4. You get notified of the decision.

### Common Approval Scenarios
- Deals above a certain amount.
- Discounts above a threshold.
- Leave requests.
- Contract modifications.

---

## 📝 Surveys

Collect feedback from customers.

### Creating a Survey
1. Click **Surveys** in the sidebar (if available).
2. Click **+ New Survey**.
3. Add questions:
   - **Text** — Open-ended answers
   - **Multiple Choice** — Pick one option
   - **Rating** — 1-5 star rating
4. **Save** and share the link with customers.

---

## 🏗️ Custom Objects

Create your own data tables for things the CRM doesn't cover out-of-the-box.

### Examples
| Custom Object | Fields |
|--------------|--------|
| Vehicles | Make, Model, Year, License Plate |
| Properties | Address, Size, Price, Type |
| Equipment | Serial Number, Location, Warranty Date |

### How to Use
1. Click **Custom Objects** in the sidebar.
2. Click **+ New Schema** to define your object.
3. Add fields (Text, Number, Date, Select).
4. Start adding records.

---

## 🏠 Assets & Reservations

Book and manage physical resources like meeting rooms and vehicles.

### How to Use
1. Click **Assets** in the sidebar.
2. Browse available assets (rooms, vehicles, equipment).
3. Click **Reserve** to book a time slot.
4. The system prevents double-booking automatically.

---

## 🌴 HR & Leave Management

Track employee leave requests.

### Requesting Leave
1. Click **HR** in the sidebar.
2. Click **Request Leave**.
3. Select: Leave type (Sick, Vacation, Personal), Start Date, End Date.
4. Add a reason.
5. Click **Submit**.
6. Your manager will approve or reject it.

### Viewing Leave Balance
- Your available leave balance is shown at the top of the HR page.

---

## 🔄 Workflows & Automation

Set up rules that automatically do things for you.

### Examples
| Trigger | Action |
|---------|--------|
| New lead added | Send welcome WhatsApp message |
| Deal moved to "Won" | Create invoice automatically |
| Ticket unresolved for 4 hours | Notify manager |
| Lead score > 80 | Move to "Hot Leads" list |

### How to Set Up
1. Click **Workflows** in the sidebar.
2. Click **+ New Workflow**.
3. Set the **Trigger** (what starts it).
4. Set the **Action** (what it does).
5. Click **Activate**.

> 💡 Ask your administrator to help set up workflows — they're powerful but require careful configuration.

---

## 📊 Workbooks (Spreadsheets)

Need a spreadsheet inside your CRM? Use **Workbooks**.

### How to Use
1. Click **Workbooks** in the sidebar.
2. Click **+ New Workbook**.
3. Add rows and columns just like Excel.
4. Use **Formulas** for calculations.
5. Data syncs with the rest of the CRM.

---

## 🌐 Web Forms

Capture leads from your website automatically.

### How It Works
1. Click **Web Forms** in the sidebar.
2. Create a form with fields (Name, Email, Phone, etc.).
3. Copy the **embed code**.
4. Paste it into your website's HTML.
5. When someone fills the form, a new lead is created automatically in the CRM!

---

*These features work together to give you a complete business management system. Explore each one at your own pace!* 🎓
